About Landed

At our core, we believe in using the power of games to make the world a better place. Our mission is to bring light into the industry by creating content that uplifts, inspires, and reflects the love of Jesus in every message we share. We are committed to breaking away from harmful industry norms such as crunch, poor mental health, and empty promises, instead prioritizing honesty, compassion, and the well-being of our team and players. Through every game we create, we strive to offer hope, joy, and meaningful experiences that make a real difference in the lives of others.
